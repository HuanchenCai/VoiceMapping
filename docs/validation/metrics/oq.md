# OQ / SPQ / CIQ — EGG glottal timing quotients

> Standard **time-based** glottal-cycle quotients from the EGG: Open Quotient
> (fraction of the cycle the glottis is open), Speed Quotient (opening vs
> closing time), Contact Index. The textbook EGG-timing family.

## 1. Implementation
- File: `voicemap/metrics.py` → `OpenQuotientCalculator` (lines 982–1044).
- Per cycle, from the EGG and its derivative:
  - `t_goi = argmin(dEGG)` — glottal opening instant (steepest decontact);
  - `t_peak = argmin(EGG)` — maximum opening.
  - `OQ  = (T − t_goi) / T`            (open fraction), clipped [0, 1];
  - `SPQ = (t_peak − t_goi) / (T − t_peak)` (opening/closing time), clip 0.1–10;
  - `CIQ = ((T − t_peak) − (t_peak − t_goi)) / (T − t_goi)`, clip [−1, 1].

## 2. Reference Standard
- **Baken & Orlikoff (2000)**, *Clinical Measurement of Speech and Voice*;
  Howard (1995) — EGG open/contact quotient definitions. These are the
  *standard time-based* quotients (distinct from the amplitude-based
  `Qcontact`, validated separately in `qcontact.md`).
- No external Python tool computes them from a raw EGG, so validation is by
  **synthetic EGG ground truth** with a known closed quotient.

## 3. Test Signals
Synthetic EGG (`_synth_egg` in the harness): a contact waveform with closed
plateau `[0, CQ]`, linear opening fall over `ramp = 0.15`, then the open phase;
the closure is the inter-cycle step (trigger at the GCI). Closed forms:
`OQ = 1−CQ`, `SPQ = ramp/(1−CQ−ramp)`, `CIQ = (1−CQ−2·ramp)/(1−CQ)`.
Swept over `CQ ∈ {0.3, 0.4, 0.5, 0.6}`.

## 4. Validation Method
- **(B) Synthetic ground truth:** each quotient recovers its closed form —

- **(A)** no reference tool. **(C)** corpus deferred (modal/breathy/pressed

## 7. Known Limitations
- **Depends on clean derivative extrema.** `argmin(dEGG)` / `argmin(EGG)` are
  unambiguous on a clean synthetic EGG; on a noisy or double-peaked real EGG
  (vocal fry, diplophonia) the GOI/peak can be mislocated, scattering OQ/SPQ.
  Gate on Clarity and prefer medians.
- **Trigger must be at the GCI.** The quotients are defined relative to the
  cycle start = glottal closure; a trigger offset (e.g. from acoustic-derived
  marks) shifts t_goi and biases OQ. The EGG phase-portrait trigger places it
  at the GCI; acoustic cc marks would need an alignment offset.
- **Clipping** (`OQ∈[0,1]`, `SPQ∈[0.1,10]`, `CIQ∈[−1,1]`) hides pathological
  values; a clipped quotient is a bound, not a measurement.
- **No absolute corpus calibration yet** — typical modal ≈ 0.4–0.6 OQ etc. is
  documented in the literature but not yet checked on a real EGG corpus.
